Our Story

A Legacy of Timeless
Hospitality

Started by Hospitality graduates who aim to promote the beauty of the city of Taguig, the Hospitium Grand Hotel was established in 2005 from a shared vision and passion for service. Inspired by well-known luxurious hotels around the world, the founders wanted to recreate the same feeling of comfort and elegance while showcasing the warmth of Filipino hospitality.

From its humble beginnings, the hotel has grown into a trusted place for guests seeking quality service and a relaxing stay. Despite its 20 years of service, the hotel has maintained a total of 41 rooms only, composed of 25 Regular Rooms, 8 Deluxe Rooms, 6 Elite Rooms, 1 Suite, and 1 Presidential Suite. This is because the management chose to focus more on quality rather than expansion, ensuring that each room is well-maintained and that every guest receives personalized attention and excellent service.

By keeping a limited number of rooms, the hotel is able to preserve a peaceful environment and provide a more exclusive and comfortable experience, reflecting its commitment to guest satisfaction and attention to detail.
